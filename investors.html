Investor Relations

AHM co-invests alongside a small group of partners on branded-hotel acquisitions in the upper Midwest. We underwrite, convert, and operate every property in the portfolio directly.

Investment Thesis

Why branded, secondary-market hospitality.

Sioux Falls and the surrounding Dakota/Iowa/Minnesota region offer what coastal markets don't: affordable entry points, steady demand drivers, limited new supply, and operating margins that hold through cycles.

Driver I

Demographic growth

Sioux Falls is one of the fastest-growing metros in the upper Midwest, anchored by an expanding healthcare, finance, and logistics economy.

Driver II

Brand-distribution moats

Marriott and Choice flags give our properties access to global loyalty programs and direct booking channels that independent hotels can't replicate.

Driver III

Value-add repricing

Converting a Super 8 to a Quality Inn typically lifts ADR 25–35% with modest PIP spend — a repeatable playbook in our pipeline.

Driver IV

In-house operating edge

Managing directly (not through third-party managers) typically preserves 300–500 basis points of NOI margin over the hold period.

Our Playbook

Acquire, reposition, operate, compound.

Four steps, applied consistently across every acquisition in the portfolio.

Acquire

Target independently owned or tired-flag assets in stable markets — underperformance is the opportunity, not the disqualifier.

Reposition

Execute the PIP, convert to a higher-tier flag (Super 8 → Quality Inn, EconoLodge → Quality Inn), and refresh the guest experience.

Operate

Take the property direct. Tight staffing, in-house ops systems, disciplined P&L review every single week.

Compound

Refinance into stabilized cash flow. Redeploy equity into the next acquisition. Grow the portfolio without diluting the standard.

Partnership Structure

How we work with investors.

We structure each acquisition as a property-level LLC with investor equity alongside our own sponsor commitment. Terms are negotiated per deal — below is the general shape.

Structure

Property-Level LLCs

Each asset is held in its own limited liability company. Investor and sponsor capital is clearly separated, and returns are distributed per the operating agreement on a per-deal basis.

Hold Period

5–10 Year Horizon

We underwrite long. Refinance events typically return capital to investors in years three to five, with ongoing distributions from stabilized cash flow through exit.

Returns

Preferred + Promote

Investors receive a preferred return on contributed capital, with the sponsor earning a promote only after the preferred hurdle is cleared — fully aligned incentives through the deal lifecycle.

Reporting

Quarterly Operator Letters

Investors receive quarterly operator letters covering occupancy, ADR, RevPAR, NOI, capex, and forward outlook at the property level. Annual K-1s delivered on standard hospitality timelines.
