How We Operate

How we operate.

These are the standards every property is held to, whether it's a 67-key Marriott or a value-tier conversion in a rural market.

Direct operations

We manage what we own. No third-party operators, no pass-through management fees, no layer between capital and property.

Brand discipline

We operate exclusively under Marriott and Choice flags. Franchise systems de-risk revenue and give us access to the best distribution networks in the industry.

Automation-forward

Labor is our largest controllable cost. We invest early in systems — from scheduling to inventory to guest messaging — that let smaller teams run bigger properties cleanly.

Long hold periods

We underwrite for ten-year ownership, not flips. That changes every decision — capex, staffing, partner selection, capital structure.
