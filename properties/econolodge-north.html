About this property

Positioned at 5100 N. Cliff Avenue on the north side of Sioux Falls, 2.3 miles from Sioux Falls Regional Airport and a short drive to the I-90 corridor. The 80-key value-tier product serves drive-up leisure travel, regional sports and youth tournament traffic, and price-sensitive business demand. Rooms include flat-screen televisions, refrigerators, coffee makers, and in-room workspace. Non-smoking and family-configured rooms are available.

The property runs an indoor pool, fitness room, continental breakfast, free wifi, and free surface parking, and accepts dogs. Managed directly by AHM's in-house operating team under the EconoLodge flag within Choice Hotels International's Choice Privileges distribution network. Downtown Sioux Falls, the Denny Sanford event arena, and North Sioux Falls industrial employers sit within a short drive.
