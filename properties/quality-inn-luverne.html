About this property

57-key asset at 801 S Kniss Avenue in Luverne, positioned on the I-90 corridor and 26.8 miles east of Sioux Falls Regional Airport. The property currently operates as an EconoLodge within Choice Hotels International's value tier, serving drive-up interstate traffic and Blue Mounds State Park leisure demand.

AHM is repositioning the asset upmarket from EconoLodge to Choice's Quality Inn mid-scale flag. The re-flag targets a higher ADR band and broadens the property's reach into corporate and healthcare demand tied to the Luverne hospital and regional employers. Room, lobby, and exterior finishes are being upgraded to Quality Inn brand standards under the conversion scope.

Amenities include an indoor pool and hot tub, free breakfast, free Wi-Fi, and free parking. Rooms offer flat-screen televisions, refrigerators, desks, and coffee makers, with non-smoking and family configurations available. Managed directly by AHM's in-house operating team, positioned alongside AHM's sister EconoLodge conversion at 1202 S Kniss Avenue in the same I-90 exit cluster.
