About this property

Interstate-adjacent asset at 1202 S Kniss Avenue in Luverne, positioned on the I-90 corridor and 26.7 miles east of Sioux Falls Regional Airport. The property currently operates as a Super 8 by Wyndham, serving drive-up interstate traffic, Blue Mounds State Park leisure demand, and regional agricultural business.

AHM is converting the asset from the Wyndham Super 8 flag to Choice Hotels International's EconoLodge flag. The re-flag moves distribution onto the Choice Privileges network and positions the property alongside AHM's sister EconoLodge and converting Quality Inn assets in the same Kniss Avenue corridor, consolidating brand presence at the Luverne I-90 exit.

Room product includes flat-screen televisions, refrigerators, and non-smoking configurations. Operating amenities include free breakfast, free Wi-Fi, free parking, 24-hour front desk, and pet-friendly rooms. Managed directly by AHM's in-house operating team, with the conversion scope covering signage, front-of-house finishes, and Choice-standard room upgrades.
