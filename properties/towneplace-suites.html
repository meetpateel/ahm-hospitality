About this property

Located at 4545 W Homefield Drive on the west side of Sioux Falls, 4.1 miles from Sioux Falls Regional Airport and adjacent to I-29. The 67-key extended-stay product serves medical travelers, corporate relocations, and project-based assignments with stays that typically run five nights or longer. Each suite is configured with an in-room kitchenette, refrigerator, microwave, and dedicated workspace. The Empire Mall retail corridor and the 41st Street business district sit within a three-mile radius.

Amenities include a swimming pool, fitness center, hot breakfast, and free surface parking. The property is pet-friendly with a $75 pet fee. Managed directly by AHM's in-house operating team under Marriott's TownePlace Suites flag and the Bonvoy distribution network, with demand anchored by Sanford Health and Avera Health medical campuses across the Sioux Falls metro.
